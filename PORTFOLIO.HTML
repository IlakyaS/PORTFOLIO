About Us

Doggy Kennel consists of 100 wonderful volunteers who are dedicated to help all animals in need. We help neglected, sick, and homeless dogs. Speaking of Doggy, Outreach missions are held on the first and third Saturday of every month. During our missions, sick and injured animals are taken in and others are fed.
